CERN Open Data Portal

This CERN Open Data portal is the access point to a growing range of data produced in the research performed at CERN. Data not already made available directly with the associated publications is minted with persistent identifiers, published and preserved here. Such data includes simplified formats as well as fully reconstructed events, together with associated software and documentation needed to access and use the data.
